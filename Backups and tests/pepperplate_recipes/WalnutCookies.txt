Ingredients:
	3/4 cup unsalted butter
	3/4 cup confectioners sugar
	1/2 teaspoon vanilla
	1 cup flour
	1 cup walnuts (finely chopped)

Instructions:
	Beat sugar and butter until light and fluffy
	Beat in vanilla
	Add flour and nuts
	Beat until blended
	Drop by teaspoon onto ungreased cookiesheet
	Refrigerate for 10 to 15 minutes
	Shape into balls and place piece of walnut on top of each (press walnut in a bit but do not flatten balls)
	Bake in 325 oven for 18 to 20 minutes
