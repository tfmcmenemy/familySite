Ingredients:
	1 1/2 pounds cube steak
	1 package brown gravy mix
	1 package au jus mix
	1 can cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup
	1 can french onion soup
	1 can water

Instructions:
	In medium bowl combine soup, water and packaged mixes. Mix well. Put cube steak in slow cooker. Pour soup mixture over cube steak. Cover and cook for 6-8 hours on low. Serve with mashed potatoes or buttered noodles.
