Ingredients:
	6 cups all-purpose flour
	3 tablespoons sugar
	3 tablespoons baking powder
	1 tablespoon sale
	1 cup frozen, grated butter

Instructions:
	In a large bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir in frozen, grated butter. Place mix in an airtight container or freezer container. Refrigerate for up to 2 weeks or freeze for up to 3 months.  Makes 7 1/2 cups
	Buttermilk biscuits - combine 2 cups make ahead baking mix and 2/3 cup buttermilk. Stir just until mixture is moistened. Turn onto surface dusted with flour. Knead 10 to 12 times or until dough holds together. Roll dough 1/2 inch thick. Cut with 2 1/2 inch round cutter. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown at 450
	Sweet muffins - Preheat oven to 400. Coat bottoms only of muffin tin with nonstick cooking spray. In medium bowl beat 2/3 cup milk, 2 tablespoons melted butter and d1 egg with wire whisk until well blended. Stir in 2 cups make ahead baking mix and 1/3 cup sugar just until moistened. Spoon batter into muffin  cups. Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until golden.
	Blueberry Scones - Preheat oven to 400. Combine 2 1/2 cups make ahead baking mix and 1/4 cup sugar. Cut in 1/4 butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Beat together 2 eggs and 1/4 cup milk. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients; stir just u til blended. Fold in  3/4 cup blueberries. Turn dough onto a floured surface; gently knead 10 to 12 strokes. Pat into a 7 inch circle. Cut into 8 wedges. Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until golden brown. Combine 1 cup powered sugar, 1 tablespoon milk and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla to brush o ver scones (This is optional)
