Title: Baked Chicken Casserole
Description: 4 skinless chicken breasts
1 envelope lipton onion soup
8 ounces sour cream
1 can cream if chicken soup

Trim all fat off chicken and place chicken in a glass baking dish. Mix all other ingredients in a bowl. Pour over chicken. Bake at 350 covered with foil for 1 hour; then uncover and bake for an additional 30 minutes. Add cheese, bacon or other toppings of your choice during last half hour.
