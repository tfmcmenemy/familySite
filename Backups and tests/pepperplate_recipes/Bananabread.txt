Ingredients:
	2 eggs
	1 cup sugar
	1 teaspoon baking soda
	1/2 cup shortening melted - I use margerine - l stick
	1/4 cup buttermilk
	2 cups unsifted flour (spooned into cup)
	3 mashed bananas
	3/4 cup walnuts (optional)

Instructions:
	In large bowl combine all ingredients except bananas; stir until flour is moistened; add bananas and stir until well blended. Pour into well greased loaf pan. Bake at 350 for 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours until brown. Remove from pan and cool on wire rack. You can also make muffins - bake them at 350 for about 25 minutes.
