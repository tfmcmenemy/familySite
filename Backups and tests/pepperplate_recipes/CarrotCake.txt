Ingredients:
	One Box Betty Crocker Carrot Cake Mix
	2/3 cup apple sauce
	1 cup water
	3 eggs
	1/4 to 1/2 cup of raisins
	1/4 to 1/2 cup chopped pecans
	1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
	1 pound of cream cheese (two 8 ounce packages)
	2 cups of confectioners sugar
	1 tub of cool whip (8 ounce)

Instructions:
	Combine cake mix, apple sauce, water, eggs and nutmeg in a bowl. Beat until well mixed. Stir in raisins and pecans. Pour into two eight or nine inch cake pans and bake according to degree and time directions on cake mix box. Cool.
	Beat cream cheese and sugar until well mixed.  Whisk in one tub of cool whip into the sugar/cream cheese mixture.   Enough to frost a two-layer cake
