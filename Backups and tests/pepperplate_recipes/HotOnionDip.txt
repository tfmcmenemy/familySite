Ingredients:
	INGREDIENTS
	2 8-ounce packages cream cheese – softened
	1 package Boursin cheese – softened
	1 1/2 cups sweet yellow onion – diced
	1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
	1/4 cup Gruyere cheese or Swiss cheese – shredded – optional, but oh so good.
	Crackers, chips or vegetables for dipping.

Instructions:
	INSTRUCTIONS
	Preheat oven to 375F.
	Spray a 2-quart shallow baking dish with cooking spray.
	Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl.
	Mix with an electric mixer until will combined.
	Pour the mixture into the prepared dish.
	Bake at 375F for 45 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
	Remove from oven – let cool a few minutes then serve with favorite crackers, chips and/or vegetables.
	Enjoy!
