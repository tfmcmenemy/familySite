Ingredients:
	2 (10 oz.) boxes frozen spinach, thawed and chopped
	1/2 (10.75 oz.) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
	1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
	3/4 cup gruyere or swiss cheese, grated
	1 egg
	2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
	1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
	kosher salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste

Instructions:
	Place spinach in 2 layers of paper towels and squeeze out all excess moisture.
	In a large bowl, mix together spinach, mushroom soup, egg and Dijon mustard until combined, then season with garlic powder, salt and pepper.
	Pour spinach mixture into a large baking dish and top with cheddar cheese and gruyere.
	Place baking dish in oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Note: cover with aluminum foil is cheese is melting too quickly.
	Remove from oven and serve hot.
	Enjoy!
