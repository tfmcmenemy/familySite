Ingredients:
	1 1/2 c flour. 1 c butter softened
	1 tsp. Baking powder. 1 c brown sugar packed
	1/2 tsp baking soda. 1/2 c white sugar
	1/2 tsp salt. 1 large egg
	1/2 tsp cinnamon 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
	1/8 tsp ground cloves, or nutmeg
	3 c oats
	1/2 c walnuts or pecans
	1/2 c or more dried cranraisins.
	Can also add 1/2 c white chips

Instructions:
	Cream butter,  gradually add sugars.  Beat in egg, then vanilla.  Stir in dry ingredients that have been blended(use wooden spoon)
	Stir in nuts, berries, chips
	Bake 350. 13-15 min.

Notes: OK these cookies were a hit by all here. FYI - I used nutmeg (not cloves) and baked cookies for about 20 minutes. I did not add chips or nuts.
