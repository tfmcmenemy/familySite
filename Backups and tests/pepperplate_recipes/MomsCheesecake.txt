Ingredients:
	FOR CRUST:
	1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
	2 tbsp. butter
	2 tbsp. sugar
	FILLING:
	1 lb. cream cheese (2 packages)
	3 eggs
	1 cup sugar
	1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
	1/4 teaspoon almond extract
	1 pint sour cream

Instructions:
	CRUST:  Melt butter and mix with crumbs and sugar; grease spring form pan and line it with crumb mixture. Refrigerate for a couple of hours.
	FILLING:  Mix all ingredients in blender until smooth. Pour into chilled crust. Bake at 375 for 1/2 hour. Leave in oven for one hour after it has been turned off. Serve chilled.
	This cheesecake is best made the day before served
