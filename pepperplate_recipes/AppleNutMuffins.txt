Ingredients:
	1 egg
	1 cup apple juice or milk
	1 teaspoon vanilla
	2 cups flour
	1/2 cup regular sugar
	1/4 cup brown sugar
	1/2 cup vegetable oil
	1 tablespoon baking powder
	1/2 cup chopped nuts
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1 apple chopped
	sugar & cinnamon (mixed for shaking)

Instructions:
	Beat egg with juice, oil and vanilla
	Stir in flour, sugars, baking powder
	Stir in nuts and apple
	Grease muffin tin and fill
	Sprinkle muffin tops with sugar//cinnamon mix
	Bake at 400 for 20 minutes
