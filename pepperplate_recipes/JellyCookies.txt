Ingredients:
	2 sticks unsalted butter
	3 egg yolks
	1/2 cup sugar
	3 cups flour
	2 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
	1 jar raspberry preserves
	Confectioners sugar

Instructions:
	Cream butter, yolks and sugar until fluffy
	Add vanilla and flour; mix well
	Roll dough dough out about 1/4 thick and cut out circles with cookie cutter
	For top half of sandwiches use a thimble to cut out hole in the middle of cookie
	Place all rounds (tops and bottoms) on ungreased cookie sheet
	Bake in a 325 oven for about 12 minutes
	After cookies are cool, spread bottoms with raspberry preserves.
	Sprinkle tops with confectioners sugar BEFORE PLACING THEM ON BOTTOMS
	Each batch makes about 30 small cookies
