Ingredients:
	2 sticks "I Can't Believe It's Not Butter"
	1 c. confectioners' sugar
	1 tsp. almond extract
	2 c. flour
	1 c. finely chopped almonds

Instructions:
	Beat "butter" with 1/2 cup of confectioners' sugar until fluffy. Add almond extract. Sift flour and add gradually to mixture, along with almonds. Shape in a ball and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
	To shape - pinch off walnut size balls of dough. Shape like a fat new moon. Bake at 350 degrees for 18-20 minutes until faintly brown. Dust when cool with remaining confectioners' sugar.
