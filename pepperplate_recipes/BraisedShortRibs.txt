Title: Braised Short Ribs

Ingredients:
	4 pounds bone-in-beef short ribs, trimmed of fat
	1 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
	1 teaspoon salt, divided
	2 tablespoons olive oil
	2 large onions, peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick
	4 carrots, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch pieces
	4 stalks celery, cut into 1 inch pieces
	4 cloves garlic, minced
	1 bay leaf
	1 cup Cabernet Sauvignon
	1/2 cup beef broth

Instructions:
	ONE:  Preheat oven to 300. Sprinkle short ribs with pepper and 1/2 teaspoon salt. In a 4 - 6 quart Dutch oven brown the ribs, half at a time, in hot oil over medium high heat. Remove ribs from Dutch oven. Add onions, carrots and celery.
	TWO:  Cover and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add garlic; cook 1 minute more.  Carefully add wine, broth, bay leaf and remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt.  Bring to a boil. Return ribs to Dutch oven.
	THREE:  Cover and transfer to oven. Braise 2 1/2 hours or until meat is tender. Stir once halfway through. Remove and discard bay leaf. Season to taste with salt and pepper
	Makes 4 servings
