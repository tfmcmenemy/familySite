Title: Chocolate Chip Cannoli Cones

Ingredients:
	15 oz. whole milk ricotta cheese
	3/4 cup powdered sugar
	1/2 tsp Vanilla
	1/4 tsp Cinnamon
	2/3 cup heavy whipping cream
	1 1/3 cup mini semisweet chocolate chips
	10 to 12 waffle cones

Instructions:
	Strain ricotta cheese using paper towels or cheese cloth set in a strainer. Strain for 30 minutes. Discard water drippings.
	Add powdered sugar, vanilla, & cinnamon to ricotta cheese. Stir to incorporate.
	Pour whipping cream into a medium bowl. Use a hand mixer to whip cream until stiff peaks form.
	Fold ricotta mixture into whipped cream.
	Stir in 1/2 cup chocolate chips
	Scoop into a zip bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
	Melt  1/2 cup chocolate chips in microwave.
	Dip waffle cones into melted chocolate. Roll in remaining chocolate chips. Set on wax paper to cool.
	Snip off one corner of zip bag with ricotta cream filling. Pipe filling into waffle cones.
