Ingredients:
	1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
	2 tbsp. butter
	2 tbsp. sugar

Instructions:
Melt butter and mix with crumbs and sugar; grease spring form pan and line it with crumb mixture. Refrigerate for a couple of hours.
