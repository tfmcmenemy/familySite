Ingredients:
	1 pound almonds or pecans
	1 egg white
	1 tsp. vanilla
	3/4 cup brown sugar
	1/4 cup white sugar
	1 tsp cinnamon
	1/2 teaspoon of salt

Instructions:
	Beat egg white and vanilla until fluffy
	In separate bowl combine sugars, cinnamon and salt
	Dump nuts into egg whites and stir to coat
	Dump sugar mixture on nuts and stir to coat
	Pour nuts onto a cookie sheet in a single layer
	Bake at 225 degrees for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
