Ingredients:
	1 cup (2 sticks) butter or margerine
	3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
	1 1/2 cups to l 3/4 cups sugar
	4 eggs
	1 teaspoon vanilla
	1 cup all purpose flour
	1/4 teaspoon salt
	3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:
	Preheat oven to 350; lightly grease an 8 or 9 inch square baking pan.
	Melt butter; in a bowl mix cocoa and sugar then stir in melted butter
	Add eggs and vanilla and stir again
	Add flour and salt;mix until smooth
	Add the walnuts
	Pour mixture into greased pan and spread out in an even layer
	Bake for about 40 to 50 minutes
	Cool for about 15 minutes before cutting and icing.
