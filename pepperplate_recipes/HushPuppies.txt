Ingredients:
	l cup corn meal
	3/4 teaspoon salt
	1 teaspoon of baking powder
	3/4 teaspoon of sugar
	1 egg
	1/2 cup of milk
	1/4 cup of onion, finely minced
	Fat for deep frying

Instructions:
	Preheat deep fat fryer to 375
	Combine dry ingredients
	Beat together egg and milk
	Add to dry ingredients
	Stir until smooth
	Blend in onion
	Drop by spoon into deep fat, keeping batter stirred
	Fry until golden brown.  Drain on paper towel
	Should make 15 to 20 hushpuppies
