Ingredients:
	1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
	1 cup confectioners sugar
	1 1/2 teaspoons almond extract
	2 cups all purpose flour
	1 cup finely chopped almonds

Instructions:
	In large bowl beat butter with sugar until light and fluffy
	Blend in almond extract
	Sift flour and gradually add to mixture along with almonds.
	Blend together until if forms a dough
	Shape dough into ball, seal in plastic and refrigerate at least 1 hour
	Heat oven to 350
	Roll dough out with rolling pin and cut with cookie cutter
	Arrange on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 18 - 20 minutes or until faintly brown
	When cookies are completely cool, dust tops with confectioners sugar
